3. Unconditional Positive Regard (UPR)
The ability to convey deep and genuine acceptance of the client as a person, regardless of their behaviors or choices. This fosters a safe, non-judgmental environment, which is the cornerstone of healing (Person-Centered Therapy).

4. Empathy & Congruence
Empathy is the capacity to accurately and sensitively understand the client's experience from their point of view. Congruence (Genuineness) means being authentic and transparent within the professional role. The client must perceive the counselor as real.

5. Theoretical Integration & Flexibility
